Cadet's Programmes

NCC Unity Run

The Unity Run was successfully conducted at Iqbal HSS on November 20, 2025, as part of the NCC Day celebrations.The event commenced with the Principal's inaugural address, followed by the Headmistress's felicitiations. She advised the cadets to prioritize their safety during th run. Two ANOs, Captian Jyothi K Nair and First Officer Nazim A R,guided the cadets during the run, which covered a considerable distance. A total of 44 cadets, comprising 24 first-year and 20 second-year cadets, participated in the event.

Community Service

The cadets actively took part in a social service activity by serving food to the community. This initiative was conducted to instill values of compassion, teamwork, and responsibility among the cadets. Their dedication and discipline were clearly reflected as they worked together to ensure that everyone was served with care and respect.
